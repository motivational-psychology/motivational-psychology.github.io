# Motivational Psychology

You don't have to let life break your spirit.

> Suffering does not come from life's challenges.
>
> It comes from what we choose to do about those challenges.

- So you have a setback.
- Is this the first time?
- Will this be the last time?
- How can you get through a setback without mental suffering?

My intent with this project is to accumulate the skills to soldier through any suffering I may have (and like anyone else, I have had my bouts) and to work through those problems and take good notes so that I may review my work and help myself in the future. I want to remain anonymous. If I put my name on this, I would tend to guard against frank honesty, which is needed to deal effectively with the actual problems. Placing my name on this could lead to inaccurate depictions to preserve my psyche, but not solve the problem. I am seeking resilience to setbacks, which will happen again. I also want skills to avoid setbacks in the first place.

A human that can navigate through life preventing the setbacks and dealing effectively through those setbacks that are not succesfully avoided can build resilience to maintain effectiveness responsiveness to navigate through life's challenges. Everyone will have their challenges. How to navigate through them is highly valuable.
